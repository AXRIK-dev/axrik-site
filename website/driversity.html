Case study — Driversity Training

Spreadsheets out.
One training hub in.

Driversity Training develops drivers and instructors across several programmes. AXRIK replaced its patchwork of spreadsheets, shared documents and email with one hub that manages every trainee, every qualified member of staff and every compliance check — then added a social studio on top so the marketing stops being the job that never gets done.

ClientDriversity Training
SectorProfessional training
DeliveredTraining hub + social studio
AI featuresPost drafts · captions · AI images

The problem

Nobody had one reliable view of anything.

Training records lived in spreadsheets. Session notes lived in shared documents. Scheduling lived in email threads. Every trainer kept things slightly differently, and version conflicts were routine — so the question "where is this trainee up to?" never had a quick, trustworthy answer.

On top of that, the standards checks that keep qualified staff compliant were tracked by hand — exactly the kind of thing that can't be allowed to slip.

What we built

One hub for the whole operation.

1 · The training management hub

  • Two tiers of people — trainees and qualified staff — each with their own records, sessions and declarations
  • Standards checks and programme tracking built to Driversity's exact qualification rules
  • Role-based logins, so every trainer sees only their own caseload and nobody else's
  • Session logging, scheduling and full history in one place — no more version-conflict spreadsheets

2 · The social studio

  • Jot a few rough notes and AI drafts the whole post, in the brand's own voice
  • AI image creation, so every post has something eye-catching to go with it
  • Posts tailored for Facebook, Instagram and LinkedIn — ready to copy straight in
  • Built into the same hub, so it's one login for the whole business

AI built in

A week of posts, written in minutes.

Drafts whole posts from rough notes, in the brand's own voice
Creates AI images to go with each post
Tailors the draft to Facebook, Instagram or LinkedIn — ready to copy straight in

Every AI feature has a manual fallback — the business never depends on AI to function, it just moves faster with it.

On the phone

The whole hub, installed like an app.

Driversity installs straight to the home screen — its own icon, opening full-screen like any native app. No app store, no download, and it updates itself every time we improve it.

Installed · full screen Driversity, one tap from the home screen Exactly as it sits on the team's phones — the full training hub, always up to date.

The outcome.

The entire operation in one place — every trainee's progress at a glance, compliance handled automatically, and the marketing that used to never happen now done in minutes instead of an evening.

New trainers get a login, not a folder of spreadsheets. And "where is this trainee up to?" now takes one click to answer.
