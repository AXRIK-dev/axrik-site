01

JG Foods · Mobile food business

An ordering site plus a business app

This business sells and delivers on a weekly cycle, and was taking every order by hand through social media messages — then copying them across several spreadsheets, all filled in manually, to work out the round. It worked, but it was hours of manual admin every week. We built two things that work together: a website customers order from, and a back-office app to run the whole operation. Orders now flow straight through, the weekly run builds itself, and the system handles the admin that used to take up so much of the owner's time.

Customer ordering site Back-office app Auto picking lists AI order parsing AI social posts

What made it bespoke

  • A customer ordering website tied to that week's live availability — sold-out items simply don't show
  • A separate business app to manage orders, customers and the weekly delivery run in one place
  • Picking lists and run sheets generated automatically from confirmed orders
  • Customer accounts and order history, so regulars are recognised and reordering takes seconds
  • Built around this business's actual products and rules — not a generic shop template

AI built in

Turns messy orders pasted from social messages into clean, structured orders
Drafts replies and updates to customers
Writes the weekly social posts from live stock

Hours of order-wrangling gone every single week. Orders land ready to pack, the Monday run sheet writes itself, and nothing slips through the net — the business runs while the owner sleeps.

02

Driversity Training · Professional training provider

A staff & training management hub

This business trains and develops people across several programmes, and was running it all on spreadsheets, shared documents and email. Nobody had a single, reliable view of who was where in their training. We replaced the lot with one hub that manages everyone — trainees working towards qualification, fully qualified staff, and the standards checks that keep them compliant — with each trainer signing in to see only their own people. Then we added a social studio on top, so marketing the business doesn't get pushed to the bottom of the list every week.

Role-based logins Sessions & standards checks Multi-programme Social studio AI captions

What made it bespoke

  • Two tiers of people — trainees and qualified staff — each with their own records, sessions and declarations
  • Standards checks and programme tracking built to this provider's exact qualification rules
  • Role-based logins, so every trainer sees only their own caseload and nobody else's
  • Session logging, scheduling and full history in one place — no more version-conflict spreadsheets
  • A built-in social studio: AI-drafted posts and images, tailored for LinkedIn, Facebook and Instagram — ready to copy straight in

AI built in

Drafts whole posts from rough notes, in the brand's own voice
Creates AI images to go with each post
Tailors drafts to each platform, ready to copy straight in

The entire operation in one place — every trainee's progress at a glance, compliance handled automatically, and the marketing that used to never happen now done in minutes instead of an evening.

03

Driversity Training · Fleet safety & driver assessment

An on-road driver assessment portal

This provider assesses other companies' drivers out on the road and reports back to the employer. Assessors were filling in paper and Word templates, and every report had to be retyped, formatted and chased before it could go out. They needed assessments to be consistent, reviewable, and turned into a professional branded report every time — and they needed to change the forms themselves without paying for a developer each time the criteria moved. We built exactly that, with compliance baked in.

Editable forms Approval workflow Auto PDF reports 5-year auto-delete AI outreach agent

What made it bespoke

  • Assessment forms the team edits themselves — add, rename or remove fields with no code
  • An approve-or-return workflow: every assessment is reviewed before it reaches the employer — and can be sent back to the trainer to edit with one click
  • Approved reports go to the client in one click — a branded PDF with a report guide attached, so the employer knows exactly how to read it
  • A built-in session guide the trainer can open at any time while filling out the assessment
  • A dashboard showing what's awaiting review, what's been approved, and what needs changes
  • Built-in data retention — approved reports auto-delete five years after the session for compliance

AI built in

Finds relevant local businesses to approach
Drafts a personalised outreach email for each one
Queues every draft for a human to review before sending

Polished, professional reports in the employer's inbox the same day instead of the same week — while an AI agent quietly keeps the new-business pipeline full around the clock.
