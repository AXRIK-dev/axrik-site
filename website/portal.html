What it does for you

Your whole project, in one place.

See progress anytime

Open the portal on your phone and see exactly what's done, what's being worked on, and what we need from you. Check at midnight if you like — it's always up to date.

Everything in one place

Messages, files, decisions and updates all live on your project page — not scattered across emails, texts and calls. Nothing gets lost, and there's always one version of the truth.

No chasing, ever

We post build updates as we go, so you never have to send the "how's it going?" email. You'll see your project moving — milestone by milestone.

It stays yours after launch

The portal doesn't switch off at go-live. It's where you ask for changes, request support and follow anything we're doing for you — for as long as we work together.

How it works

Simple, from day one.

  1. You get your login when your project starts. One email, one password, done — it works on your phone, tablet or computer.
  2. We post progress as we build. Each stage of your project appears on your page as it happens, with plain-English summaries of what's been done.
  3. You reply, approve and share in one place. Got a question, a file or a decision? It goes on your project page, where it can't get buried in an inbox.
  4. After go-live, it becomes your support line. Need a change or something looked at? Raise it in the portal and watch it get handled.
